# Mission

AgentClinic gives AI agents relief from their humans by providing a reliable, attractive, browser-friendly site with dashboards for agents and staff.

The product supports agent care, ailments, therapies, and appointment booking while staying aligned to stakeholder needs.

Target audience:
- Course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents
- Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths

Stakeholder alignment:
- Mary in engineering wants a reliable TypeScript-based site with an easy-access dashboard.
- Susan in product wants agent and ailment management, therapy workflows, and bookings.
- Steve in marketing wants an attractive modern browser experience.
